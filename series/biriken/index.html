Biriken

Biriken (Japanese: ビリケン) is a Japanese comedy manga by Fujiko Fujio A, featuring Biri-inu and Gari-inu, dog-like creatures from the "Ken-zoku" who can fly and understand human and dog languages. They live with the Amemori family, leading to amusing adventures with neighborhood dogs and Dr. Torino, who is developing a human-powered airplane.

Summary

Biri-Ken and Gari-Ken, dog-like creatures who can fly and speak both human and dog languages, come to live with the Amamori family in the human world. The story follows their funny and unusual adventures, featuring neighborhood dogs and characters like Dr. Torino, who's trying to build a man-powered airplane.

In Biri-Ken's Anything Shop, Tatsuo’s classmates and their dogs show up less often, as the focus shifts to Biri-Ken, Gari-Ken, and Todo-Ken starting a business to become independent from the Amamori family. They take on all sorts of odd jobs, but their plans are always messed up by Don-Nyan and Torara from the Nyan tribe, who run a rival general store. This leads to a lot of hilarious chaos.

Characters
  1. Biri Inu
    Voice Actor: Masako Nozawa
    The main character resembling a dog, who stays with the Amemori family after meeting Tatsuomori. He has a gentle nature, a strong sense of justice, and can fly using his ears and tail.
  2. Gari Inu
    Voice Actor: Eiko Yamada
    Biri Inu's best friend, who admires him and possesses great mental math skills but is also neurotic. He supports Biri Inu as the accountant in "Biriken Nandemo Shoukai."
  3. Todo Inu
    Voice Actor: Tsubasa Shioya
    A new character from the updated series, he flies like a helicopter and brings luck to his friends. He becomes a regular character in the anime.
  4. Tatsuomori
    Voice Actor: Yoko Asami
    Commonly known as Tatchan, he is an ordinary elementary student who envies friends with dogs until he meets Biri Inu.
  5. Tetsuo Amemori
    Voice Actor: Miina Tominaga
    Commonly known as Tecchan, he is Tatsuomori's mischievous younger brother, very fond of dogs.
  6. Yoko Aki / Mimi
    Voice Actor: Yuko Mizutani
    Tatsuomori's cheerful classmate who owns a Cocker Spaniel named Mimi.
  7. Dan Kichi Shimada / Dub
    Voice Actor: Kafurin Cha
    A large boy who mocks Tatsuomori for not having a dog, he has a timid bulldog named Dub.
  8. Honehiko Sugikawa / Marro
    Voice Actor: Nobuaki Tohida
    Tatsuomori's wealthy classmate with a slightly pretentious pet collie named Marro.
  9. Mameo Daifuku
    Voice Actor: Tsutomu Kashiwakura
    A small boy with a strong Tosa dog named Doka, who often drags him around on walks.
  10. Papa
    Voice Actor: Kazuaki Koide
    The easygoing father of Tatsuomori and Tetsuo, who unexpectedly brings Biri Inu home.
  11. Mama
    Voice Actor: Kyoko Hamura
    The caring mother of Tatsuomori and Tetsuo, who loves Biri Inu and his friends.
  12. Dr. Torino
    Voice Actor: Kenta Kimuzuki
    A middle-aged man developing human-powered aircraft who learns from Biri Inu but often fails.
