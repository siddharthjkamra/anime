Mami the Psychic

Mami the Psychic (エスパー魔美, Esupā Mami, "ESPer Mami") is a manga created by Fujiko F. Fujio, first released in 1977 through Shōnen Big Comic. The narrative centers on Mami, an ordinary middle school girl who unexpectedly uncovers her range of psychic abilities. As she navigates her daily life, she uses her newfound powers to aid those around her, encountering various challenges and adventures along the way. The series blends humor and supernatural elements, showcasing Mami's growth as she learns to harness her gifts for good.

Summary

Mami Sakura was once an ordinary junior high school student until she unexpectedly gained supernatural powers. With the help of her childhood friend, Kazuo Takahata, she investigates and resolves mysterious events.

Using her psychic abilities, such as telekinesis and telepathy, Mami comes to the aid of those in distress. When she senses someone in need, she activates the "Teleportation Gun," a heart-shaped brooch designed by Takahata, to transport herself to the location. This teleportation is triggered only when a projectile is flying toward her, making the locket a portable gadget that allows for controlled teleportation rather than random occurrences. Her telekinesis is remarkably precise, enabling her to extract cancerous cells from one of her father's art critics. Additionally, her telepathy grants her access to people's thoughts and dreams.

Characters
1. Mami Sakura (佐倉 魔美, Sakura Mami)
Voiced by Keiko Yokozawa. The chief character, a student with awakened supernatural powers, using them for kindness. She possesses telekinesis, levitation, telepathy, and teleportation, often with humorous mishaps.
2. Kazuo Takahata (高畑 和夫, Takahata Kazuo)
Voiced by Hiroyuki Shibamoto. Mami's best friend and a genius who helps her develop her powers. He has a strong sense of justice and often supports Mami.
3. Konpoco (コンポコ, Konpoko)
Voiced by Akari Hibino. Mami's pet dog, resembling a raccoon dog, who understands human words and enjoys fried bean curd.
4. Juro Sakura (佐倉 十朗, Sakura Juurou)
Voiced by Hiroshi Masuoka. Mami's father, a laid-back painter with a passion for art.
5. Naoko Sakura (佐倉 菜穂子, Sakura Naoko)
Voiced by Yoshiko Sakakibara. Mami's mother, who works at Asauri Newspaper and often critiques Mami's abilities.
6. Hosoya (細矢さん, Hosoya-san)
Voiced by Yumi Nakatani. A local gossip who lives in the same town as the Sakuras.
7. Satoru Takenaga (竹長 悟, Takenaga Satoru)
Voiced by Nozomu Sasaki. Mami's classmate and a baseball friend of Takahata, known for his strong sense of justice.
8. Noriko Momoi (桃井 のり子, Momoi Noriko)
Voiced by Yuriko Fuchizaki. An optimistic friend of Mami, known for her cooking.
9. Sachiko Mamiya (間宮 幸子, Sachiko Yukiko)
Voiced by Hiroko Emori. Another friend of Mami, described as calm and possibly Takenaga's girlfriend.
10. Tsuyoshi Banno (番野 兆治, Banno Tsuyoshi)
Voiced by Yoku Shioya. A delinquent whose actions inadvertently trigger Mami's powers.
11. Takashi Tomiyama (富山 高志, Tomiyama Takashi)
Voiced by Yoshikazu Hirano. A classmate of Mami who is a classical music enthusiast.
12. Narihiro Arihara (有原 成宏, Arihara Narihiro)
Voiced by Hirotaka Suzuoki. Director of the movie club at Tobita Junior High School, planning a controversial film.
13. Shohei Kurosawa (黒沢 庄平, Kurosawa Shouhei)
Voiced by Nobuo Tobita. Vice Director of the movie club who suspects Mami's abilities.
14. Taeko Kuroyuki (黒雪 妙子, Kuroyuki Taeko)
Voiced by Hiromi Tsuru. A childhood friend of Takahata who enjoys riding motorcycles and disco.
